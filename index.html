About this taxonomy

This lexicon maps legacy beauty industry terminology to its longevity science equivalents across four registers: legacy term, longevity reframe, scientific basis, and plain language. The taxonomy draws on peer-reviewed literature in geroscience, cosmetic dermatology, and longevity medicine. Scientific basis descriptions are grounded in the references below. They do not constitute medical advice. This is a living document, updated as the evidence develops.
